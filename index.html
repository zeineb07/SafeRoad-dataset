About the Dataset

This dataset is designed for advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS). It was collected from different Moroccan cities and focuses on detection and classification of pedestrians in eight walking directions: Front, Back, Left, Right, Left-Front, Left-Back, Right-Front, and Right-Back. Data was captured using an industrial camera with a resolution of 2.3 megapixels at 60 frames per second, mounted on a moving vehicle. The dataset supports training and evaluating state-of-the-art models with multiple annotation formats (TXT and COCO JSON).

Benchmarks

We evaluated three state-of-the-art detection architectures on the SafeRoad dataset — a two-stage detector (Faster R-CNN), a real-time single-stage detector (YOLOv8), and a transformer-based end-to-end model (DETR) — to provide a standardised baseline for future comparisons.
